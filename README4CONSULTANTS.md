# NextEco for consultants and technical auditors

NextEco is useful in consulting because it turns a fuzzy question into a reviewable artifact:

> What does this system cost to run, and how sure are we?

## Why this matters in consulting

Clients often ask for:
- cost clarity
- infra clarity
- AI credibility
- sustainability signals
- a way to compare scenarios

NextEco gives you a lightweight deliverable that can live in the client repository instead of staying trapped in a slide deck or spreadsheet.

## What you can do with it

- audit a repo quickly
- document one representative request or job
- compare local and external API costs
- make uncertainty explicit
- leave behind a maintainable artifact after the consulting mission

## Consulting-friendly output

The generated Markdown is readable enough for:
- engineering teams
- reviewers
- procurement-adjacent conversations
- delivery documentation
